Keto Beef Egg Roll Slaw

Description

A savory, low-carb twist on takeout, this Keto Beef Egg Roll Slaw combines seasoned ground beef, crisp cabbage, and bold Asian-inspired spices into a quick, one-pan meal—no wrapper needed!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • ½ cup diced onion
  • 5 green onions, chopped, white and green parts separated
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 ½ pounds ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon chili-garlic sauce (such as sriracha)
  • ½ teaspoon ground ginger
  • sea salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 (14 ounce) package coleslaw mix
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
