Keto Chicken Parseman

Description

This Keto Chicken Parmesan replaces traditional breadcrumbs with a cheesy, almond flour–Parmesan crust, then bakes tender chicken breasts topped with low carb marinara and melty mozzarella. With only 6g total carbs, 47g protein, and 442 calories per serving, it delivers all the comforting flavors of classic chicken parm in a keto-friendly package.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) skinless, boneless chicken breast
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon heavy whipping cream
  • 1 ½ ounces pork rinds, crushed
  • 1 ounce grated Parmesan cheese
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon red pepper flakes (Optional)
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon Italian seasoning/li>
  • 1 tablespoon ghee (clarified butter)
  • ½ cup jarred tomato sauce (such as Rao's®)
  • ¼ cup shredded mozzarella cheese
