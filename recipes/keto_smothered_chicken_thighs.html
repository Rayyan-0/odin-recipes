keto Smothered Chicken Thighs

Description

This Keto Smothered Chicken Thighs dish features juicy, bone-in chicken thighs seared in a skillet with bacon and mushrooms, then finished in the oven. A rich, creamy gravy made from chicken broth and heavy cream brings it all together, delivering a comforting, low-carb meal—with just 2g net carbs per serving and around 466 kcal—that's both hearty and keto-friendly.

Ingredients

  • 4 (8 ounce) skin-on, bone-in chicken thighs
  • 1 teaspoon paprika/li>
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 slices bacon, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
  • ⅓ cup low-sodium chicken broth
  • 4 ounces sliced mushrooms
  • ¼ cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2 green onions, white and green parts separated and sliced
